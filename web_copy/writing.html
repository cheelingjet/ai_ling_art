CHILD

Here it is: your paper; it is an untouched canvasa silent piano waiting for its melodies; play it; let it dance; fold it and unfold it; let it breathe in and out; it will come to life; now watchwatch how the other children make their craneseach corner neatly overlapping the other in crease after crease, fold after fold; watch the way their hands move swift and smooth across the paper; look at the child who uses tape; dont use tape to make up for what youre lacking; fix the wrinkles; stick to the book; no glueing, no cutting; make cranes, tigers, swans, or dragons; your animals must be majestic, powerful, and most of all, respectedbeautiful, beyond what you can imagine; no wrinkles allowed; child; look at the others; look at the colors and patterns they use; it will determine what kind of creature you bring to life; dull paper, dull creature; dont make such simple and degrading animals; you mustnt make your origami so bland by just using black or white; this is how you match colors to the animalsto make them more vivid when they come to life; you are the creator; they are your creations; craft them like you would a dream: alluringelusive; this is how you make a swan graceful as a ballerina; this is how you make a tiger fierce and powerful as a predator; your animalsthey will only become failures when they come to life; child; unfinished and unfulfilled; dont let them be; this is how you dispose of animals that didnt come to life; this is how you choose the correct animal to bring to life; this is how you must care for your animal so it does not die early; make something you can be proud of; child; this is how you laugh when your dragon comes to life; this is how you laugh when your dragon soars into the sky; after all this time that I have guided you, taught you, corrected you, if you cannot bring your origami to life, then the problem must be the animal, and not you.

Sand,
sneaking between
my toes,
burying me in
a sun-kissed
warmth. I could
melt.

Swallow me whole,
I want to say.
I want to know.
What is it like...
living under the
clouds, the
gleaming sun,
and crystal moon?

You dance with blue,
an empty floor,
all day long.
For one night,
wont you be my
partner?

Sea,
flooding my senses,
cradling me in a
shimmering
embrace. I could
drown.

The whistling breeze
brushes me to you.
I'm not much of a
swimmer,
but you'll keep me
afloat won't you?
