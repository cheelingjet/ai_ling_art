Time Travel

This collage named "time travel," offers a blend of analog and digital techniques. Originally crafted as an analog collage, I handpicked clippings from miscellaneous magazines. Then the artwork embarked on a digital odyssey through Photoshop and Procreate where it underwent metamorphosis.

Time travel is an exploration of regret and a desire to turn back time. It takes on the form of a celestial landscape reminiscent of the night sky or galactic expanse, creating a sense of wandering within time and space. At the heart of the collage lies a mirage of eyes, gazing in opposing directions to mirror the act of looking forward and back in time. Cast with dimly glowing stars, dancing figures, and scatter paper airplanes, the piece is illuminated with the wish for temporal liberation.

BLUE DESIGN SERIES

These two design projects were part of a series aimed towards designing primarily with the color blue:

What Music? is an infographic that investigates personal music taste trends in 2023. The horizontal axis delineates the time of day and the vertical axis maps the chronological progression from fall to spring. 7 genres are visually represented with their own shapes and colors: pop, rock, folk, jazz, classical, R&B, and hip hop.

BUILD is a typography experiment inspired by LEGOs that transforms geometric shapes—circles, triangles, squares, stars, etc—and recognizable patterns to reimagine the English alphabet, breaking it apart and reconstructing it.

STAPLED

Stapled, like the accompanying poem I wrote with it paints a vivid picture of colors breaking free with its shimmering quality. I wanted to explore a more tactile experience.


With a stapler in hand, I began to staple my portrait. Each puncture felt freeing, like breaking through the seams of the artwork with the strong, sharp clasps.The big wash of purple, blue, green, yellow, orange, and red hues symbolizes the release of emotions—a sense of liberation and vibrant self-expression that mirrors the sensation of making or thinking of making art.
